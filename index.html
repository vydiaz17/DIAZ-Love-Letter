To my Dearest Closest Friends,

Thank you for being the best people I've ever met. Since the day we've met, I've always felt like I finally belonged somewhere.
For the past 4 years, we've grown together as people, and we've been through a lot.
I've always struggled with dealing with myself, I never got to know myself and I've always tried fitting in with others around me.
I constantly bottled everything in, thinking that it would be better off being how everyone expects me to be rather than being honest.
But because you decided to reach out to me, and told me everything would be fine, and what I felt was valid, I realized I could open up to others bit by bit.
Even though I'm still struggling with finding my identity, I'm glad that I have you all with me, because I truly wouldn't want it in any other way.
Thank you for always being there, and for being true friends.

Love, Venice M. Diaz
